Non-Technical Events

  • Individual participation only.
  • Duration:20 minutes.
  • Participants should try to make each other laugh.
  • participants will watch a series of hilarious video clips, memes, and live comic acts — and the goal is simple: don’t laugh, smile, or even smirk..
  • Last one standing wins!.
  • Team of 2 members.
  • One will enact(Speaker), the other will guess(Guesser).
  • Every correct guess gets 1 point. If they can't guess, they can skip and move to the next word.
  • No lip movement allowed.Use of signs and body language only.
  • Switch Roles: After one round, players can switch roles ;Guesser becomes Speak for the next round. Winning: The team with the most correct guesses in the given time gets the chance to participate in the final level
  • 2 members can participate.
  • Audio-based guessing game — no visuals;Listen to audio clips from real ads (TV/online);Clip types may include:Only jingles (music);Famous voices/dialogues;Background ad sounds (brand names removed)
  • Each clip is 10–30 seconds long;Participants must raise their hand and answer within 15 seconds.
  • Correct brand/product = 10 points
  • Multiple rounds with increasing difficulty (Easy → Medium → Hard);Tie-breaker round if needed
    Optional Rapid-Fire Final Round (shorter, quicker clips)
    Team or individual with the highest total score wins or qualifies for finals
